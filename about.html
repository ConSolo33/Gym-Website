GLOBAL GYM

Welcome to Global Gym, your new home for local athletic activies. Located centrally in downtown Lakewood, Global Gym has numerous facilities which include 2 basketball courts, a full size swimming pool, a rock climbing wall, and a fully functional weight room. Open 7 days a week, there are plenty of activities available on a daily basis. There are times reserved daily for open access to the facilities, as well as structured class times for all skill levels. Please sign in below in order to view more information about our gym as well as access the monthly schedule for all facilities!
