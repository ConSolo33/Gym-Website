Activities

Rock Climbing

Global Gym provides three rock climbing walls of differing difficulty, ranging from beginner to advanced. Each wall contains a mixture of artificial and natural holds that are perfect for any skill level. Each of the three walls are also equipped with belaying equipment to provide safety while climbing. In addition, there will be staff available to provide assistance with belaying upon request. In order to belay without a staff member present, you will have to provide certification of completion of a climbing safety course. Climbing safety courses are available on site monthly. Please reference the schedule for more details.

Weight Room

Global Gym has two adjacent weight rooms on premisis, one focused primarily on free weight / body weight exercises while the other contains primarily machines. Within each of these weight rooms you will find lockers, towels, and water fountains so that you have everything you need in one place. Weight rooms are open daily for free use, however if you would like to schedule a guided session we do have trainers available to book. There are also class sessions including yoga, body weight training, strength training, and cardio training available. Please reference the schedule for more details.

Swimming Pool

Global Gym has an Olympic size swimming pool on site that is available for free use daily. There is always at least half of the pool reserved at all times for lap swimming, while the other half is used some of the time for open swim. Pool depth ranges from 4 feet to 12 feet deep. There is also a diving board at the deep end of the pool. During open swim sessions, a lifeguard is present at all times. There are also certain hours that are reserved for adult-only swim sessions. Please reference the schedule for more details.

View Schedule

Basketball Gym

At Global Gym, there are two adjacent full-court basketball courts that can be separated or used at the same time via a controllable barrier. Additionally, there are hoops interspersed against the walls throughout the gym with differing heights that can be raised and lowered upon request. There are open gym sessions almost every day available at certain times, while there are also scheduled activities including volleyball, rec basketball, and pickleball weekly. In order to browse open gym times, or sign up for any activities, please reference the schedule.
